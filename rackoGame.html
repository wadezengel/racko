Rack-o!

To win Rack-o you must have all ten cards in your rack in order from least (at the bottom) to greatest (at the top).

An additional requirement to win 2-player Rack-o, you must also have 3 cards in squential order. (Example: 22, 23, 24).

On your turn you must either draw from the deck or the discard pile and then swap the card with a card in your rack.

If you drew from the draw pile you may also choose to place the card directly in the discard pile.
